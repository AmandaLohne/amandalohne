Old but new chair

Purpose - why did I do it?

I wanted to display my plants. Make them a part of the interior of the apartment. Hopefully give them more light simultaneous. Wanted to learn more about the 3D-printing process. Learn more about how to use Fusion 360. I got the inspiration from the internet. Just be searching "climbing plant support" you get several different ideas of how to make your plant climb up a wall. I fell in love with the idea to make a clip that looks like a leaf, so it is not so easy to spot the support mechanism. The "picture idea" I found on the internet had green filament leafs with midrib and veins on the top part of the clip. I thought it would be an relative easy get-to-know-how-things-work task to find inspiration (a design), make it similar to it and figure self how to cadde it, and in this case how the closing mechanism works.
